Things to Do in Cayucos

Make a weekend vacation out of our wedding! Cayucos is beautiful in May and there are plenty of sights to see and things to do. Below are some things you can experience in Cayucos.

TOUR THE HEARST CASTLE

Hearst Castle is an over-the-top homage to material excess, perched high on a hill. The estate sprawls across acres of lushly landscaped gardens, accentuated by shimmering pools and fountains, statues from ancient Greece and Moorish Spain and the ruins of what was in Hearst’s day the world’s largest private zoo (look for zebras grazing on the hillsides of neighboring Hearst Ranch). To see anything of this historic monument, you have to take a tour (try to book ahead).

SIP WINE IN PASO ROBLES

Paso Robles is about 40 minutes North-East of Cayucos, and it's packed full of vinyards and olive orchards. Stop by for an afternoon of tasting!

EXPERIENCE THE LOCAL CULTURE

Morro Bay, Cambria, and San Luis Obispo are extremely accessible from Cayucos and offer their own kind of fun! You can hike nature trails, kayak with otters, eat fresh seafood, and shop for the perfect antique. And of course, there is always the beach!
